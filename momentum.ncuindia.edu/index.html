MOMENTUM2016

Momentum is all about fest where we provide mass and you are the velocity. The festival lasts for three days which includes some professional performances and a host of other activities. From the brain storming sessions in quizzes and debates to exihilarating events like fashion show and histrionics. About NCU

Located in the National Capital Region, The NorthCap University is a boutique university, committed to building competencies in engineering, management, law and sciences, apart from research and exploring newer applications of industrial relevance. The University’s value based, inter – disciplinary and industry ready curriculum, quality intellectual faculty resource, dedicated staff, research focus, and a superior enabling and supporting physical and academic infrastructure firmly establishes it in the league of top private universities of India and a most preferred education and research destination.
